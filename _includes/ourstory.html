Our Love Story...

Dillon and Chelsea met in the magical city of Austin, TX. Dillon had lived in Austin for a few years after college and was currently working as a software engineer at iFly, while Chelsea moved there after college to work for College Forward while applying to medical schools. The two met at the start of Chelsea's year and hit it off, which gave Dillon the opportunity to show her all the best spots in Austin, including Zilker Park and McKinney Falls.

The year flew by as the two bonded over many a taco and the year was coming to a close before Chelsea was to begin at Jefferson Medical College in Philadelphia, PA. The two decided to jet-set to New Zealand to celebrate the year and to get some fun in before Chelsea started school. They had a blast tasting wine, exploring Hobbiton, and bungee jumping, all in the gorgeous green backdrop of New Zealand. Soon after their adventure in a foreign land, Chelsea started at Jefferson and found herself immersed in a new world full of memorization and anatomy lab.
